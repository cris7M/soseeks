About Soseeks

Soseeks India Pvt Ltd is a fast-growing technology company headquartered in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh — at the heart of India's emerging tech corridor along IIT Bhilai Road. We are builders, trainers, and innovators committed to empowering businesses and individuals through technology across India.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity. Through our software training programs, we prepare the next generation of developers across Bhilai, Raipur, Durg, Bilaspur, and beyond. Through our development and digital marketing services, we help businesses — from ambitious startups to established enterprises across India — ship products that make an impact.
