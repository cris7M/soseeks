What is Chat2Bill?

Chat2Bill is retail billing software and a point-of-sale (POS) system designed for Indian businesses. Instead of tapping through long product menus, users describe the sale in text — for example weights and item names — and the software matches products, applies GST (CGST/SGST), and produces a professional tax invoice. Stock levels update automatically so owners can trust inventory between physical counts.

The product is aimed at high-volume, low-margin shops where speed and clarity matter: general stores, medical stores, mobile accessory counters, stationery, and similar formats across metros and smaller cities.

Who is Chat2Bill for?

  • Kirana & general stores — fast billing with HSN-aware lines where applicable.
  • Pharmacy & medical shops — structured lines, batch-oriented workflows where you configure them on the main product.
  • Electrical, hardware & building-material counters — variable SKUs and rate cards supported through your catalogue setup.
  • Owners who want a dashboard — today’s sales, weekly trends, top movers, and low-stock signals in one place.

Why we built it (Soseeks)

Many small shops still juggle registers, calculators, and generic apps that are not built for Indian GST practice or inventory discipline. Soseeks builds Chat2Bill so billing feels as familiar as messaging, while the back end enforces tax lines, stock movement, and printable invoices — including support for thermal printers where you use them in-store.

Key features (summary)

  • Chat billing — natural text (e.g. “2 kg sugar, 1 Maggi pack”) builds the cart and invoice.
  • GST-ready invoices — CGST/SGST, HSN-aware lines, print or share.
  • Inventory & alerts — stock moves with every sale; low-stock visibility.
  • Dashboard — daily and weekly sales, top products, orders — desktop and mobile in sync.
  • Built for India — works on budget Android, light payloads per bill, Android app on the official site.

How billing works (high level)

1. Catalogue

You maintain products with rates, tax slabs, and HSN codes — import from spreadsheet where supported on chat2bill.com.

2. Sale

Staff type the sale in the chat-style billing screen; the app resolves lines and taxes.

3. Invoice & stock

A GST tax invoice is generated; inventory decrements; the dashboard reflects the transaction.
