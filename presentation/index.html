Our Story

Built From Real Operational Challenges

Soseeks India Private Limited was founded with a practical approach toward solving operational problems faced by Indian businesses.

Instead of building isolated custom software repeatedly for individual clients, Soseeks focuses on identifying recurring business challenges and converting them into scalable SaaS products.

The company is building modular business automation platforms that simplify workflows, reduce operational complexity, and improve accessibility for SMEs and enterprises.

Soseeks leverages modern automation technologies, conversational workflows, cloud infrastructure, and intelligent systems where they create meaningful business value.

RagBook

Enterprise Knowledge & Workflow Intelligence Platform

RagBook is an enterprise-focused platform designed to help organizations manage internal knowledge, workflows, documents, and operational intelligence. The platform leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture for intelligent enterprise search and contextual information retrieval.
